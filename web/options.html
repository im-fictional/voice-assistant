VOICE ASSISTANT COMMANDS:

OPENING OF PROGRAMS

Searches for the program you specified and launches it.

OPENING OF SITES

Opens the first link of the said site.

SEARCH ON THE INTERNET

Doing a search for what you said.

VOLUME CHANGE

Change the volume to what you say.

SHUTDOWN/REBOOT/SLEEP

On your command, it will turn off, reboot or enable sleep mode on the device.

WHAT TIME IS IT?

It will tell you the current time.

WRITE/SEND TEXT

Write/send the text you say.

CREATING A DOCUMENT ON THE DESKTOP

The command creates a text document on
your desktop.

CREATING A FOLDER ON THE DESKTOP

The command creates a folder on your
desktop.
